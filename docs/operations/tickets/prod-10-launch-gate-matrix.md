## Scope Notes

1. This matrix governs publishable launch claims for the current window.
2. Tool rows cover the prompt-first launch surface, not richer direct-tool dispatch that exists behind the runtime/controller contract.
3. Image rows are bounded to single-image attach/contextual Q&A, not broader multi-image or document-analysis promises.
4. Voice is available as a production opt-in in release builds. It remains outside the broad public claim set tracked here until retained multi-OEM and 24-hour evidence supports wake, background-reliability, and battery claims.
5. Single-image rows are only claim-safe when the required multimodal companion artifact is covered by setup/provisioning evidence, not just by the visible attach UI.
6. `AI human-proxy` packets may satisfy required rows whose only remaining dependency is the moderation-backed WP-13 packet for the controlled MVP, but only when the packet is explicitly disclosed as proxy-derived, uses the approved bundle/setup path, and is paired with current machine-verifiable evidence.
7. `S-F` retains its controlled-MVP pass for context-preserving guided recovery. The pre-fix July probe required Refresh; `18fe52bf` restores retryable readiness, but `UX-13` remains a separate publication-closeout requirement until the exact two-send regression is retained and green.

## Pilot Promotion Checklist

Required for `promote`:

1. All required rows = `PASS`.
2. No open `UX-S0`/`UX-S1` blockers.
3. Latest evidence ids recorded for `android-instrumented`, hosted/default runtime-readiness coverage, and `journey`; direct S22 AndroidJUnitRunner canary proof may substitute for wireless Maestro while Maestro remains harness-class only.
4. Latest journey send-capture stage reports `phase=completed` and `placeholder_visible=false`.
5. A `human-moderated` or disclosed `AI human-proxy` WP-13 packet contains measured values (no `not collected` fields).
6. Privacy-related public claims map only to `Verified` entries in `docs/operations/tickets/sec-02-privacy-claim-parity-audit.md`.

Advisory for scope sizing:

1. `first_useful_answer_ms` trend.
2. Onboarding + recovery completion time distribution.
3. 7-day scorecard keep/iterate/stop recommendation.
